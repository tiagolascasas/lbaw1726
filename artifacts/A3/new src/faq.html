FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is BookHub?

BookHub is a web platform that allows EU residents to register and pariticipate in online book auctions. The auctions are limited to literary items and can last from 5 minutes to 7 days. The payment is processed through Paypal.

What should I know before I start participating in book auctions?

After registration, you can participate in book auctions as a buyer and as a seller. You must follow the rules concerning each of these roles described in this FAQ or you may subjected to a ban by the moderating team. Only one registred account is allowed per person.

What should I do if I want to participate in an auction as a buyer?

As a buyer you are allowed to bid in other user's auctions. If you win the auction, you have 48 hours to pay for the item through Paypal. You must have a verified Paypal account in order to pay for the item, otherwise you shouldn't participate in any auction until you have a verified Paypal account. Any misbehavior may result in a permanent ban.

What should I do if I want to create an auction and participate as a seller?

As a seller you are allowed to create new auctions and wait for other users biddings until the auction ends. You are not allowed to bid your own auctions. A new auction will only be made public after it's been aproved by the moderating team. After the auction closes, the winner has 48 hours to confirm the payment and after the payment, you should ship the item using a method that provides tracking information after the payment is confirmed. You should receive the payment to your Paypal account within 48h of dispaching and sharing the tracking information. The buyer is allowed to leave a feedback in your profile account regarding his experience.

What should I do if I bought a book but it never came?

You must give 40 full days after the shipping of the book. After that time, if you still haven't receive the book, message the moderating team about your issue and you will be contacted within 48 hours through your specified e-mail adress.

The auction of my book has ended but the winner didn't pay, what should I do?

You should give 48h after the auction is over. If after 48h the winner doesn't pay, he will be contacted and subjected to a ban depending on the sittuation, to be decided by the moderating team. You will be allowed to create a new auction for the same item.

What information should I leave as a feedback?

As a buyer, after a purchase, you are expected to express your apreciation or depreciation related to your purchase experience. After the positive or negative feedback choice you can leave a customized message that can detail the experience related to the shipping, packaging, assemblence of the item to the decription and communication with the seller.

How do I contact the seller if I want more information about an item?

Registred users are allowed to visit other user's profiles and to view their e-mail information. You can contact a user through their respective e-mail adress if you want to ask him to add more information to an item.

I created an auction but made some mistakes, can I edit the auction after it's already active?

You are allowed to edit your auction if the time to end is higher than 1 day but the edit must first be aproved by the moderating team. After the edit is aproved, the auction will be reset as new, starting new it will have no bids and the desired auction time will be reset. If the edit is not in time to be aproved by the moderating team or the time left for the auction to end is less than 24 hours, you, as a seller will have the obligation to sell that item as described and face your mistakes or be subjected to the consequences to be decided by the moderating team. If you abuse your right to edit, you may be subjected to a permanent ban.
